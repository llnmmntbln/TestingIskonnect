Accessibility FAQs for Tanauan Iskonnect

Account Management

To apply for scholarship or educational assistance, you must first create an account. After creating an account, you'll need to wait for an email notice.

To create an account, on the upper right side of the page click the "Login" button. After clicking the login button, click the "Create Account" button and fulfill the credentials needed for creating an account.

After logging in, on the profile button, find "edit profile", after changing your desired information, click save changes. For profile picture it is required to use only an ID picture with white background.

On the login/sign up page, click on the 'Forgot Password' and the administrator will give you an email with instructions on resetting your password.

    On your profile, you'll get to know your scholarship status according to the color of your profile picture border.


  • Green - active and existing scholar
  • Yellow - scholar with pending requirements or for requirements renewal
  • Red - failed grades or eliminated scholar
Requirements Submission

For documents, you must only upload a ".pdf" file. While for images, you may submit images in ".jpg",".jpeg",".png" file format.

  • As an applicant, you may click the Applicant requirements button on the left side of the page and upload the required documents.
  • As a maintainer, you may click the Renewal requirements button on the left side of the page and upload the required documents.

  • Educational Assistance Form - To be asked from Barangay
  • Certified True Copy of Previous Grade
  • School Registration Form
  • Certificate of Good Moral
  • Certificate of Residency

You may be uploading a large file. This website only accepts file that has maximum space of 25MB.

Scholarship Approval

You will get a notification via gmail if you we're accepted or not.

You may still apply if one of your siblings finished scholarship programs, but if you have a failing grade you may not be able to apply again.

Releasing of Grants

You can check it on the schedule of events located below the homepage.
